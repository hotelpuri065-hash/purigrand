Our Story

Hospitality Rooted in Puri

Puri Hotel Grand stands on Shankar Road, minutes from the temple town's most cherished landmarks — the Jagannath Temple and the Bay of Bengal shoreline. Our building rises above the neighbourhood, giving many of our rooms sweeping views of the sea and city.

We built this hotel around a simple idea: every guest, whether on a spiritual journey, a family holiday, or a quick beach getaway, deserves a clean, comfortable room and a team that genuinely cares. From our restaurant to our front desk, that idea shapes everything we do.
